Mao Shan Wang

Mao Shan Wang (MSW)

Prepare your senses for the king of all kings - Mao Shan Wang. With its golden-yellow flesh and unmistakably rich, buttery texture, this is the durian that aficionados dream of. Bold and unapologetically intense, every bite delivers a luxurious blend of bittersweet creaminess.

Encased in a green husk marked by its signature starburst at the base, the Mao Shan Wang does not just stand out - it rules.

Taste & Texture: Flesh is mostly yellow in colour, bittersweet yet creamy, buttery and smooth, sometimes with a slightly fibrous texture, mostly small seeds.

Status: Malaysia's most coveted durian, often imitated, never duplicated.

Country of Origin: Malaysia - Kelantan, Pahang and Johor.

Season: From June to August
